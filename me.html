About Us

A non-profit, non-stock institute composed of brothers and sisters and lay partners who share their time, skills and gifts to make contemplative spirituality available to all. We conduct retreats, talks, self-processing, and contemplative silence aimed at developing loving kindness, wisdom, and inner peace based on shared learning and spiritual practices.

We envision a society of enlightened and awakened persons for the restoration of wholeness and oneness of creation. We commit to nurturing the self, Mother Nature and the community. We promote healthy lifestyle, discipline and practices towards emotional balance and transformative spirituality.


What we do

HOLISTIC HEALING & INNERCISES

We teach various healing modalities and exercises to support one’s spiritual practice and contemplative life.

MINDFULNESS CULTIVATION

Mindfulness exercises aimed at achieving inner balance, broader understanding of God, human life, and the whole of creation.

LECTURES & TALKS

Facilitate discussion on subjects that help promote understanding and harmony among various faiths, traditions, and practices.

PERSONALITY AWARENESS

Personal retreats to process awareness of one’s personality and give opportunity for healing using available tools such as enneagram and journal writing.

RETREATS

Short term and residential retreats to ground and heal oneself from everyday environment. Conducted to uplift one’s spiritual needs by tapping available learnings & practices of sages, saints, and spiritual mystics.
